doe_factorial

Computes a factorial design of experiments

Calling Sequence

H = doe_factorial(nb_var)

Parameters

nb_var

the number of variables for the design of experiments

H

the created design of experiments

Description

Computes a factorial design of experiments.

Examples

model_inter = '1 x1 x2 x1*x2';

x = -1:0.1:1;
y = -1:0.1:1;
[X,Y] = meshgrid(x,y);

// Test of the factorial DoE on a linear model
H1  = doe_facrtorial(2);
RM1 = build_regression_matrix(H1,model_inter);

disp(RM1)
